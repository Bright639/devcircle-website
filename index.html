Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are some of the most common questions our clients ask.

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically 7-14 business days depending on project scope. Simple websites can be completed in a week, while complex applications may take up to a month. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you offer monthly maintenance?

Yes, we have monthly maintenance plans starting at KES 2,000. Our maintenance services include security updates, content updates, backup management, performance optimization, and technical support.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes. We allow 50% upfront, 50% after project delivery. For larger projects, we can arrange custom payment plans to suit your budget and cash flow requirements.

What tools do you use?

We use modern technologies including React, WordPress, Next.js for development, Figma and Adobe Creative Suite for design, Google Analytics and Search Console for SEO, and various marketing tools for digital campaigns.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes, we offer reliable hosting solutions with 99.9% uptime guarantee. Our hosting packages include SSL certificates, daily backups, security monitoring, and 24/7 technical support.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach, ensuring they look and function perfectly on all devices - smartphones, tablets, and desktops.

Do you provide SEO services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive SEO services including keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, content strategy, and ongoing SEO maintenance to improve your search rankings.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Certainly! We can redesign your existing website to improve its appearance, functionality, and performance while preserving your existing content and SEO rankings where possible.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
